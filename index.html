Nicat Məlikov is an Azerbaijani composer, music producer, and music video director. With a background in philology and extensive experience in the music industry since 2005, he has worked with renowned artists such as Rauf Əhmədov, Aygün Kazımova, Röya Ayxan, Sevda Ələkbərzadə, and Samir Cavadzadə. Nicat specializes in songwriting, producing, and directing music videos, blending creativity with technical expertise.
