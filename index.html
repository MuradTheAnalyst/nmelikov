Nicat Məlikov

Nicat Məlikov is an Azerbaijani music producer, composer, and director born on June 1, 1986, in Baku. With a background in philology and over 15 years of experience in music and video production, Nicat has collaborated with prominent Azerbaijani artists such as Rauf Əhmədov, Aygün Kazımova, Röya, Sevda Ələkbərzadə, and Samir Piriyev. Known for his work in both music videos and TV projects, he has contributed significantly to Azerbaijan’s contemporary music scene.
